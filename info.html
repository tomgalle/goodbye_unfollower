About Goodbye Unfollower
Goodbye Unfollower is a service for Twitter users to send a farewell poem their Unfollowers. If you received a Goodbye Poem and don't like it or want more info about it, please contact us, and include the twitter handle.

Goodbye unfollower in no way intends to insult anyone. The poems are filled with humor, and create an fun moment for both the user and the unfollower. Find out more, or try it out here.
